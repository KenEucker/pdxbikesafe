Thursday Night Ride is a community of riders that meets every week to explore Portland. Our community is diverse, friendly, and social. We welcome all riders and expect anyone joining our community to adhere to our principals and values. Members of our Safety Council are dedicated to being good stewards of bike fun around Portland. Riders are expected to be safe, transparent, and foster a comfortable ride for all.

Community Guidelines

Have fun

We want every one who shows up at the fountain to feel safe and welcome to express what makes them smile.

Ride safe

Stay behind the ride leader, remain in the center of the lane, don't pass on the left of the group without calling it out, and keep off the sidewalk please.

Pack it in Pack it out!

Whatever you bring with you must be taken out with you. If you brought it in your bag, then you can take it with you in your bag.

Don't be an ass

I don't care if you identify as hee-haw, please display respect for those around you.

Consent for everyone!

Everyone deserves to be respected and to have their boundaries respected. In the spirit of making the Thursday Night Ride a safe space for all ages, shapes, sizes, colors, sounds, and smells: be kind!

Plan A Ride



Thursday night rides can be lead by anyone! If you have an idea of where you want to go to on a Thursday, be it; an event, a park, a cool view of the city, or a spot on the river -> we want you to take us there! We created the map below, an overlay of Portland, to; highlight hazards eg: roads with train tracks, show points of interest, offer example routes that have enjoyable features, and to offer our experiences with group bicycle riding in Portland back to the community that likes to be involved.
