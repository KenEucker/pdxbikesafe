Thursday Night Ride is a community of riders that meets every week to explore Portland. Our community is diverse, friendly, and social. We welcome all riders and expect anyone joining our community to adhere to our principals and values. Our safety committee is dedicated to being stewards of biking around Portland. Riders are expected to be safe, transparent, and foster a comfortable ride for all.
